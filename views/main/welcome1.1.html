Category

ROCK

A genre of popular music that originated as "rock and roll" in the United States in the early 1950s, and developed into a range of different styles in the 1960s and later, particularly in the United Kingdom and the United States.

RAP

A genre of music, now widely considered to be a subgenre of Hip hop music, in which a performer speaks syncopated rhyming lyrics, often, but not always, over a musical rhythm and/or melody accompaniment.

ELECTRO

A genre of electronic music which originated in the early 1980s, Electronic dance music (also known as EDM, dance music, club music, or simply dance) is a broad range of percussive electronic music genres.
